Broccoli Cauliflower Casserole

This broccoli cauliflower casserole is a creamy, cheesy side dish that the whole family will love. Made with no condensed soup, this recipe is easy to make and is a great way to get kids to eat their veggies!

Broccoli Cauliflower Casserole Ingredients

  • 1 head cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 1 head broccoli, cut into florets
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 tablespoon butter, melted

    • Steps

    1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
    2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add cauliflower and broccoli and cook until tender, about 10 minutes. Drain and transfer to a 9x13-inch baking dish.
    3. Melt 1/4 cup butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Whisk in flour, salt, and pepper until smooth, about 5 minutes. Slowly pour in milk, whisking constantly until smooth, about 5 minutes. Stir in Cheddar cheese until melted, about 3 minutes. Pour cheese sauce over cauliflower and broccoli.
    4. Combine bread crumbs and 1 tablespoon melted butter in a bowl; sprinkle over cheese sauce.
    5. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 30 minutes.
